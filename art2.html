Mona Lisa

Artist: Leonardo da Vinci

Year: 1503–1506 (with later refinements)

Category: Renaissance Portrait

Medium: Oil on Poplar Wood Panel

Description

Mona Lisa is one of the most famous paintings in the world. Created by the Italian Renaissance artist Leonardo da Vinci, the portrait is admired for the subject's mysterious smile, lifelike expression, and masterful use of light and shadow. Today, it is displayed in the Louvre Museum in Paris and attracts millions of visitors each year.
