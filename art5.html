The Persistence of Memory

Artist: Salvador Dalí

Year: 1931

Category: Surrealism

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Description

The Persistence of Memory is one of Salvador Dalí's most famous surrealist paintings. It features melting clocks draped over a dreamlike landscape, symbolizing the fluidity of time and the mysterious nature of dreams and memory. The artwork challenges conventional perceptions of reality and has become an enduring icon of surrealist art.
