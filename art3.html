The Scream

Artist: Edvard Munch

Year: 1893

Category: Expressionism

Medium: Oil, Tempera & Pastel on Cardboard

Description

The Scream is one of the most recognizable works of art in the world. Painted by Norwegian artist Edvard Munch, it depicts a figure standing on a bridge with an expression of intense anxiety while the sky glows with dramatic colors. The painting symbolizes fear, loneliness, and emotional turmoil, making it a masterpiece of the Expressionist movement.
