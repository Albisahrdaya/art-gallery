Girl with a Pearl Earring

Artist: Johannes Vermeer

Year: 1665

Category: Baroque Portrait

Medium: Oil on Canvas

Description

Girl with a Pearl Earring is one of Johannes Vermeer's most celebrated paintings. Often referred to as the "Mona Lisa of the North," the artwork portrays a young girl wearing an exotic dress, a blue-and-gold turban, and a large pearl earring. The painting is admired for its lifelike expression, delicate lighting, and timeless beauty.
